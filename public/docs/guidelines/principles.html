Principles

Like all athenahealth products and services, we use our corporate purpose, commitments, and guiding principles to help make decisions when designing and building product experiences.

Design Solution Checklist

Product creators can use the following checklist to ensure product design solutions embody athena’s guiding principles.

Guiding Principles

Design Checklist

Radically simple

Hack away at the inessentials.

Healthcare is complicated. Let’s simplify it. We strip communications, messages, and experiences down to the essentials.

  • Limits the information the user is required to provide to only what is absolutely necessary
  • Focuses on primary use cases and avoid skewing to edge-cases
  • Works seamlessly with other tasks, workflows, and products
  • Utilizes Forge visual styling that recedes and does not unnecessarily call attention to itself

Refreshingly candid

Tell it like it is. Kill the jargon.

Jargon and corporate-speak can be confusing. Let’s be transparent. We make clear, honest communication the core of every interaction.

  • Uses well-established, standard, healthcare terminology and language of users rather than athena-speak, tech language, or jargon
  • Respects the sensitive and sometimes serious nature of healthcare by employing a more subdued, restrained, and respectful tone
  • Reflects patient safety needs and importance appropriately
  • Keeps users informed through appropriate feedback within reasonable time
  • Clearly describes system activities while allowing users to easily adjust selections, decisions, or choices when needed
  • Honestly states and presents the athena POV where appropriate

Powerfully relevant

Do what matters most. Predict what matters next.

Let’s connect people to what they need, when they need it. We unite network data with purposeful design to emphasize what’s most relevant now and what’s going to be relevant next. We deliver targeted insights and actionable takeaways that nobody else can offer.

  • Limits the user’s need to recall information by making appropriate content, objects, actions, and instructions easily retrievable
  • Endeavors to be usable without support but provides proper help and documentation where and when needed
  • Uses machine learning to create a personalized experience or allow users to customize their experience where appropriate
  • Includes behavioral tracking instrumentation to gain internal understanding of users’ behavior

Pushing boundaries

Tirelessly challenge the status quo.

Our industry is ripe for disruption, and we’re ready to bring unexpected changes that disarm and delight.

  • Identifies the boundaries of safety and work within them to create user experiences that exceed expectations
  • Automates all work the system can complete to do work on behalf of the user
  • Delivers value in an unexpected way to reduce the burden
